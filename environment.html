Artist's Environment
*The student as an artist*
Janwillem Schrofer writes about the artist and his environment, from victim to free spirit. In these last years, students had the opportunity to showcase their works for different types of classes. Most projects that will be showcased will be within the KABK walls. But this doesn't make it any less special! The galleries of the school are mainly used for presentation of works, and you have endless opportunities on how you would like to show your project. Going out in The Hague is definitely not something you want to miss out on.
How have you experienced these past three years?

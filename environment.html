Artist's Environment
Janwillem Schrofer writes about the artist and his environment, from victim to free spirit. During your studies, you will have multiple opportunities to showcase your works to an audience. In your second year, for example, you can choose Design Practice. You will work with real clients, real projects and have the opportunity to see your creation in the real world! I created a project for Filmhuis which is now visible on their Instagram and will later be printed on A3 posters. In the third year, there will be a Roots exhibition. This will be a huge production that relates to your roots and merges Image and Philosophy class. Anything is possible, as Janwillem Schrofer says, as long as you put your mind to it.
